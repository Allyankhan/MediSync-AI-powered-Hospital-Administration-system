City General Hospital - General Information and Policies

Visiting Hours:
- General Wards: 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily.
- Intensive Care Unit (ICU): strictly 11:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Only immediate family members are allowed in the ICU.
- Maternity Ward: Partners can visit 24/7. Other visitors are welcome from 2:00 PM to 7:00 PM.

Parking:
- Visitor parking is located in the East Garage. The rate is $3 per hour, with a maximum daily cap of $15. 
- Valet parking is available at the Main Entrance for a flat fee of $20.

Cafeteria:
- The main cafeteria is located on the ground floor, open from 6:30 AM to 9:00 PM.
- Vending machines are available 24/7 on every floor near the waiting lounges.

Medical Advice Disclaimer:
Administrative staff and this automated system cannot provide medical diagnoses, prescribe medications, or offer post-operative care adjustments. For medical emergencies, please press the nurse call button or dial 911 if outside the hospital.
